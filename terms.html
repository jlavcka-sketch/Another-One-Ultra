1. Organizer

Another One Ultra is organized by FPE Nutrition s.r.o. ("the Organizer"), based in the Czech Republic. By registering for or attending the event, participants agree to the terms set out below.

2. Registration & lottery

Registration takes place via a public lottery. Entry into the lottery does not guarantee a starting spot. The Organizer reserves the right to select participants at its sole discretion from the pool of eligible entries.

Selected participants will be notified by email and must complete payment of the registration fee within the timeframe communicated to them. Failure to do so will result in forfeiture of the spot, which may be reassigned to another applicant.

3. Fees & refunds

The registration fee covers the organization, logistics and operation of the event. Once a participant's spot is confirmed and the fee has been paid, no refunds will be issued, regardless of the reason for non-participation, including illness, injury, or withdrawal.

Starting spots are personal and non-transferable. They cannot be sold, gifted, or transferred to another person.

4. Participation requirements

All participants must be at least 18 years old on the day of the event. Each participant must sign a liability waiver prior to the start. The full waiver document will be made available to selected participants ahead of the event.

Participants are responsible for their own health, fitness and equipment. The Organizer strongly recommends consulting a medical professional before participating in an ultra-endurance event of this nature.

5. Format & rules

The event follows the Backyard Ultra format: a 6.7 km loop, starting on every full hour, continuing until only one participant successfully completes a loop within the hour. Any runner who fails to complete the loop in time, or who does not start the next loop, is eliminated.

The final remaining participant must complete one additional solo loop to be declared the official winner.

Mandatory gear (including a working headlamp during hours of darkness) must be carried at all times. Failure to comply may result in disqualification.

6. Crew & support

Each participant may have up to three crew members. Crew may assist participants only within the designated base camp area. Any support provided on the course itself — including handing over food, water, or equipment — will result in immediate disqualification (DNF).

7. Media & image rights

By participating in or attending the event, participants grant the Organizer the right to photograph, film, and otherwise record their participation, and to use such material for promotional, marketing, and media purposes across all channels, including social media, without separate compensation.

8. Liability

Participation is at the participant's own risk. The Organizer, its partners, sponsors and staff shall not be held liable for any injury, illness, loss or damage arising from participation in the event, except where caused by gross negligence or willful misconduct.

9. Changes to the event

The Organizer reserves the right to modify the event format, schedule, location, or rules if necessary due to safety, weather, or other circumstances beyond its control. In the event of cancellation, the Organizer will communicate next steps to all registered participants, but is not obligated to issue refunds (see Section 3).

For any questions regarding these terms, please contact us at info@anotheroneultra.com or via our contact page.
