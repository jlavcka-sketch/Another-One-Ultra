1. Who we are

This website and the Another One Ultra event are operated by FPE Nutrition s.r.o. ("we", "us", "our"), based in the Czech Republic. We are the data controller for the personal information described in this policy.

2. What information we collect

When you register for the lottery, contact us, or sign up for partnership inquiries, we may collect:

  • Name and surname
  • Email address
  • Country of residence
  • Any information you voluntarily provide in messages or forms
  • Payment-related information (processed by our third-party payment provider — we do not store full payment card details)

3. How we use your information

We use your personal data to:

  • Manage the lottery and selection process
  • Communicate with you about your registration, including confirmation emails and registration codes
  • Process payments for confirmed entries
  • Respond to inquiries sent via our contact or partnership forms
  • Send updates about the event, where you have agreed to receive them

4. Legal basis for processing

We process your personal data on the basis of your consent (when you submit a form), and/or to perform our obligations under the registration agreement once you are selected and confirmed as a participant.

5. Data sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share limited information with trusted third parties strictly for the purposes of running the event, such as payment processors, email service providers, and event logistics partners. These parties are required to handle your data securely and in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

6. Media & image rights

Photos and video footage taken during the event may feature participants and may be used for promotional purposes, as described in our Terms & Conditions.

7. Data retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes described above, or as required by law (for example, for accounting and tax purposes).

8. Your rights

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten"), subject to legal retention requirements
  • Object to or restrict certain processing
  • Request a copy of your data in a portable format
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at info@anotheroneultra.com.

9. Cookies

This website may use essential cookies required for basic functionality. We do not currently use third-party advertising or tracking cookies.

10. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please reach out via our contact page or email info@anotheroneultra.com.
